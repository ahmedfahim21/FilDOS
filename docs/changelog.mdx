## FilDOS 0.1.0-beta

The first public beta — an AI-native file browser that runs entirely on your
device.

<Frame caption="The FilDOS file browser.">
  <img src="/images/home.jpg" alt="The FilDOS file browser" />
</Frame>

### Browse & manage files

- List, grid, and gallery views, with thumbnails and resizable columns.
- Create, rename, copy, move, duplicate, and delete — with undo.
- Drag files in and out of Finder / Explorer.
- Changes on disk show up instantly.
- Each folder remembers how you like to view it.

### Organize

- Colour-coded tags that stick to files as you move them.
- Jump back to what you touched recently.

### Search by meaning

- Find files by what they say, not just their name — text, code, PDFs, DOCX,
  and images.
- Search your photos with words, powered by CLIP.
- Narrow results with scope and time filters.

### Ask AI

- Chat with your files using an AI model that runs fully on your device.
- Mention files and folders, or run `/find` right in the conversation.
- Let it create, move, rename, or tidy up files — you stay in control.
- Pick from many local models, or bring your own.

### Canvas

- See how your files connect in an interactive knowledge graph.
- Scrub through time to watch your files evolve.

### Cloud drives

- Browse Google Drive and Dropbox right next to your local files.

### Private by default

- Search, chat, and everything AI run on your device — nothing leaves your
  machine unless you connect a cloud drive.
- Hide any file, folder, or file type from AI with one click.

</Update>
